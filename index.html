Welcome to mProject.

This is a collaborative project which goal is to visually tell the history of Madagascar Island through pictures and interactivity. Visual content is one of the most universal content that can reach different cultures no matter the education level. I decided to launch this project as my first collaborative project so people around the world know where we Malagasy people come from. Ceci est un projet collaboratif dont le but est de raconter l'histoire de l'île de Madagascar à travers les images et l'interactivité. De nos jours, le contenu visuel est l'une des voie de communications les plus universelles, transcendant ainsi les differentes cultures sans tenir compte du niveau d'éducation.
